Meet Ricki

Personalized care, clear advice, and serious follow-through.

Ricki believes every client should be informed and comfortable in their home-buying process. Her work is rooted in honesty, local knowledge, and the quiet confidence that comes from helping people through major life transitions.

She specializes in new and pre-owned homes across Manatee and Sarasota counties. Buyers count on her for guidance, sellers count on her for persistence, and past clients often return because of her reliability.

When a repair question, neighborhood concern, insurance wrinkle, or service-provider need appears later, Ricki is the kind of agent who still answers. That lifelong mindset is central to how she builds relationships.
